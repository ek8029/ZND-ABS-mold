# Mold Remediation Website — Design Brainstorm

## Design Approaches

<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** Industrial Safety Meets Clinical Precision  
**Core Principles:**  
1. High-contrast dark navy + stark white — authority and urgency  
2. Asymmetric layouts with bold diagonal cuts between sections  
3. Oversized typography for emergency messaging  
4. Grid-breaking photo placements with hard-edge frames  

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0B1F3A) as the dominant tone evokes trustworthiness and professionalism. Urgent amber (#F59E0B) for CTAs and emergency elements signals danger and immediate action. White (#FFFFFF) for breathing room and clinical cleanliness.  

**Layout Paradigm:** Offset-column layout — hero text left-anchored, image bleeds to the right edge. Sections alternate between full-bleed dark and white with diagonal SVG dividers.  

**Signature Elements:**  
1. Bold diagonal section cuts (clip-path) between dark and light sections  
2. Oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03) as background decorative text  
3. Hard-bordered "emergency badge" components  

**Interaction Philosophy:** Immediate, no-nonsense. Hover states are quick and sharp. No playful animations — only purposeful transitions that reinforce urgency.  

**Animation:** Entrance animations via Framer Motion — text slides in from left, images fade up. Sticky header transitions from transparent to dark navy on scroll.  

**Typography System:** Barlow Condensed (headings, bold/700) + Source Sans Pro (body, 400/600). Headings in ALL CAPS for emergency feel. Body text at 17px for readability.
</idea>

## Selected Approach

**Chosen: Industrial Safety Meets Clinical Precision**

Deep navy authority + amber urgency + clinical white. Asymmetric layouts with diagonal section cuts, oversized typography, and hard-edge emergency components. Barlow Condensed + Source Sans Pro typography system.
